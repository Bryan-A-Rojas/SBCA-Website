Buwan ng Wika 2017

08.31.2017

Ipinagdiwang ng College of Arts and Sciences ang Buwan ng Wika na may temang "Filipino: Wikang Mapagbago" sa ilalim ng pamumuno ni Bb. Marcelita Balila ngayong ika-31 ng Agosto sa Fr. Roberto De Jesus Auditorium, St. Maur Hall. Nagpakita ng iba't ibang talento ang mga mag-aaral sa Sabayang Pagbigkas upang patunayan ang naaangking galing at husay ng Pilipino. Ang unang gantimpala ay nakamit ng BACMS - 3B na sinundan ng BspEd - 1A para sa ikalawang parangal at BACMS - 3C para sa ikatlo.

Mr. and Ms. NCAA 2017

08.26.2017

Appearing in NCAA's annual pageant, Angel Dannah Cardoza (III-BACMS) and Adriel Tan (III-BAIS) defined the identity of Bedan pride by means of exquisite appeal and wit. Despite the pair not being able to place a hook on the coveted titles, Cardoza managed to bag a few awards for the team--one of which was the 'Best in Formal' wear which featured the crowned representative sashaying in SBCA's signature color across the stage. She has also been regarded as Ms. Friendship in another highlight category, as well as an awardee of the McDonald's choice
