Animo Night 2017

01.01.2017

Idinaos sa Fr. Bellarmine Baltazar Gym noong nakaraang Biyernes ang libreng Animo Night na may temang Rock Back to School 8 ng SEC katulong ang Red Lines Production. Nakisayaw at nakikanta ang mga Bedans nang magtanghal ang mga inimbitahang banda kagaya ng 6 Cyclemind, Moonstar 88, Autotelic, Gracenote, Banda ni Kleggy, Stellar, Hilera, VIIXI at Imago.

Paskong Bedista 2016

12.21.2016

Ending the year with music from local artists, the Student Executive Council spearheaded a new era for Paskong Bedista at the SBCA Football Field last Dec 15. Bamboo headlined the event alongside Callalily, Banda ni Kleggy, Ang Bandang Shirley, Grace Note, Autotelic, Jensen & the Flips, Oh Flamingo, and DJ Marc Marasigan.

Buwan ng Wika 2016

11.11.2016

Bilang pagkilala at pagpugay sa wikang Filipino, idinaos ng Kolehiyo ng Sining at Agham ang Buwan ng Wika magmula Agosto 5 hanggang Setyembre 2 na may temang "Filipino: Wika ng Karunungan."

Swimming

10.30.2016

Bedans of the CAS community enrolled in their PE 103 classes officially closed the semester through the Culminating Activity held at the SBCA Complex on October 7. The swimming activity, which served as the Final Examination for the course, showcased the paricipants' techniques and skills learned during their Individual and Dual Sports classes.
