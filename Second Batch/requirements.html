ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

College of Arts and Sciences

 

 

Freshman

 

1.)  Admission fee: PhP550.00 (non-refundable and to be paid at the Cashier, Finance Department).
 
2.)  Documents to be submitted upon application:
 
2.1  ( ) Duly accomplished application form
 
2.2  ( ) Recommendation obtained from either the High School Principal, Class Adviser, Mentor, Discipline Head or Guidance Counselor. (Use SBCA Student Academic and Discipline Appraisal Form)
 
2.3  ( ) Certified true copy of the current report card (form 138). Only students currently enrolled in grade 12 may apply.
 
2.4  ( ) Two (2) recent 2”x 2” pictures in color w/ white background with applicant’s name written at the back.
 
2.5  ( ) Photocopy of birth certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (formerly National Statistics Office)
 
2.6  ( ) Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate (if baptized Catholic)
 
2.7  ( ) San Beda College Alabang Envelope (with name of applicant in PRINT at the upper left hand corner) Ex. DELA CRUZ, JUAN JR. P.
 
The Admissions and Testing Center reserves the right to decline applications that have not met the criteria set by the Board of Admissions. Incomplete documents will not be endorsed to the Board of Admissions for further evaluation.
 
Note:    Applicant may be required to submit other documents not mentioned above on a case to case basis upon evaluation of admission application. 
All documents submitted shall not be returned and shall become the property of the school. 
Original Final Report Card signed by the school principal or registrar must be submitted to the registrar’s office upon confirmation of enrollment.
 
Transferees
 
1.)  Admission fee: PhP550.00 (non-refundable and to be paid at the Cashier, Finance Department).
 
2.)  Documents to be submitted upon application:
 
2.1  ( ) Duly accomplished application form
 
2.2  ( ) Recommendation obtained from either the Academic Dean, Professor, Class Adviser, Mentor, Discipline Head or Guidance Counselor. (Use SBCA Academic and Discipline Appraisal Form)
 
2.3  ( ) True copy of grades for evaluation
 
2.4  ( ) Description of all courses/subjects taken from previous school
 
2.5  ( ) Two (2) recent 2”x 2” pictures in color w/ white background with applicant’s name written at the back.
 
2.6  ( ) Photocopy of birth certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (formerly National Statistics Office)
 
2.7  ( ) Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate (if baptized Catholic)
 
2.8  ( ) San Beda College Alabang Envelope (with name of applicant in PRINT at the upper left hand corner) Ex. DELA CRUZ, JUAN JR. P.
 
            The Admissions and Testing Center reserves the right to decline applications that have not met the criteria set by the Board of Admissions. Incomplete documents will not be endorsed to the Board of Admissions for further evaluation.
 
Note:    Applicant may be required to submit other documents not mentioned above on a case to case basis upon evaluation of admission application. 
All documents submitted shall not be returned and shall become the property of the school. 
If qualified, transfer credential or honorable dismissal or certificate of eligibility to transfer is a requirement for enrollment.
